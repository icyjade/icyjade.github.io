25 March 2020: First post. Taking Control in Uncertain Times

(1 weeks into Malaysia's COVID-19 lock down)

is no longer guaranteed.

To deal with uncertainty, I choose to take control, by upskilling myself.
And with how digital is the obvious beneficiary of this COVID-19, I embark myself to learn this subject.
